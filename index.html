About Me

Born in Portugal in 1995, I am a French-Portuguese researcher passionate about neuroscience and machine learning. My academic journey has taken me from Lisbon to Bordeaux, then to Lausanne, and now Copenhagen. Each step has deepened my understanding of behavioral neuroscience, computational analysis, and the neural basis of stress resilience. Currently, I’m pursuing a PhD in Neuroscience, focusing on understanding resilience mechanisms in zebrafish larvae.
