About Me

Hi, I’m João Henrique Campagnolo, a Neuroscientist and Bioengineer based in Copenhagen. I’m currently pursuing a PhD in Neuroscience, exploring the neural mechanisms underlying stress resilience using zebrafish as a model. My work combines techniques in whole-brain imaging, behavioral analysis, and ML to uncover insights into stress-related disorders like anxiety and depression.

Beyond academia, I enjoy learning about, and working with tools that bridge neuroscience and ML. When I’m not diving into research, I enjoy playing sports (namely tennis and football), and experimenting with whacky projects, like PillProphet (which leverages AI to predict drug trial outcomes), or rating kebabs across Europe...
